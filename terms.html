Digital Associates Program - Zyvara

1. Program Overview & Investment

  • Program Enrollment: To become a Digital Associate, you must complete the official registration process. Upon acceptance, you will gain access to the Zyvara platform and USA residential VPS.
  • Program Investment: Your one-time enrollment fee of 5,500 BDT secures your position in the program. This investment is directly allocated to providing you with exclusive access to a dedicated USA-based residential VPS environment.
  • Renewal Fee: To maintain access to the USA residential VPS environment and ongoing earning opportunities, an monthly renewal fee of 5,000 BDT is required.
  • Official Payments: All payments must be made exclusively through the secure payment channels provided on the official Zyvara website.

2. Your Role & Our Partnership

  • Associate Responsibilities: As a Digital Associate, you are expected to perform tasks such as surveys and micro-tasks with a high degree of accuracy and timeliness using our USA residential VPS.
  • Minimum Commitment: We require a minimum commitment of 3 months to ensure you have adequate time to familiarize yourself with the USA VPS tools.
  • Career Growth: Zyvara is invested in your growth with a clear pathway: Intern → Junior Associate → Associate → Senior Associate.
  • Training & Development: Completion of mandatory training programs and skill assessments is required for success on the platform.

3. Salary Settlement & Salary Cycle

  • Transparent Earnings: Your salary is calculated based on tasks you complete that pass our quality assurance verification using the USA residential VPS.

Structured Salary Cycle

We provide a structured salary cycle to ensure timely and predictable income for our USA VPS Bangladesh associates:

First Cycle (Advance Salary)

Earnings accumulated from the 1st through the 7th of the month are processed for payment around the 15th of the same month.

Second Cycle (Monthly Salary Settlement)

Earnings accumulated from the 8th through the last day of the month are processed for payment around the 7th of the following month.

  • Secure & Automatic Payments: Salary payments are processed automatically to your registered payment method.
  • Payment Verification: Zyvara verifies all task completion for quality before any earnings are approved for payment.
